Threat-Level Protection (TLP) Compliance Requirement

Overview • Security & Compliance

As part of an ongoing, platform-wide security enhancement initiative, Wix has implemented a mandatory Threat-Level Protection (TLP) compliance verification process, through which all hosted websites are systematically assessed to ensure alignment with updated security standards designed to mitigate increasingly sophisticated cyber threats.

What is Threat-Level Protection (TLP)?

Threat-Level Protection (TLP) constitutes a comprehensive framework of advanced security configurations and preventative controls that are intended to safeguard websites against a broad spectrum of vulnerabilities, including unauthorized system access, data interception, and malicious exploitation.

When properly implemented, TLP safeguards sensitive user data such as authentication credentials, form submissions, and payment information.

Importance of Compliance

Failure to maintain a properly configured TLP environment may significantly increase exposure to security breaches, leading to unauthorized access, operational disruptions, and loss of user trust.

Compliance is mandatory for all websites.

Required Actions

If TLP Has Already Been Configured

Users must submit their configuration files for verification to confirm compliance via compliance@support.wix.com.

If TLP Has Not Yet Been Configured

Users are required to initiate setup immediately. Failure to comply may result in temporary suspension until verification is completed.

Assistance and Support

Users with a flagged website and requiring assistance may connect with a specialist using the contact button for guided support to ensure proper implementation of all required configurations.

Compliance Deadline and Enforcement

All flagged websites must complete verification within the designated timeframe. Failure to comply may result in enforcement actions, including suspension, without further notice.

Platform-Wide Applicability

This requirement applies universally and supports the platform’s commitment to maintaining a secure and reliable hosting environment.

Summary

Users are strongly advised to act promptly to verify their TLP status and complete all required actions to ensure uninterrupted service.
